# Privacy Policy for CardioGuard

**Last Updated:** December 20, 2025

Thank you for using CardioGuard, a research project application for the early detection of heart disease. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected.

## 1. Information We Collect

CardioGuard collects the following health-related data that you voluntarily provide in the Risk Calculator form:
- Age
- Sex
- Chest Pain Type
- Resting Blood Pressure
- Cholesterol
- Fasting Blood Sugar
- Resting Electrocardiographic Results
- Maximum Heart Rate Achieved
- Exercise Induced Angina
- ST Depression (Oldpeak)
- Slope of the peak exercise ST segment
- Number of major vessels (ca)
- Thalassemia (thal)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used **solely** for the purpose of calculating the heart disease risk assessment. The data is sent to our backend server to be processed by our machine learning models.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

We do **not** store, save, or share any of your personal or health-related data on our servers or in the application after the prediction is made. The data is processed in real-time and discarded immediately after the prediction result is sent back to your device.

The application itself does not store your assessment history.

## 4. Third-Party Services

CardioGuard does not use any third-party services that would collect your personal data.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
